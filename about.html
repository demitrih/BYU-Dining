About BYU Dining


BYU Dining Services nourishes the campus community in a spirit of hospitality which supports learning, enhances community, and builds character. BYU Dining Services strives to fulfill this responsibility through the purchase and use of products which are safe for consumption, support local sustainability, and demonstrate a commitment to stewardship.
